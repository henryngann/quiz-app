How to Use

1. Upload File

2. Submit File

3. Wait for processing (typically ~5s)

4. Click the Quizlet Button!

Results

The question sets produced by this app are stored in Quizlet after the processing is complete. The app produces 2 types of questions: a property question or a fill in the blank question. A third type, diagram questions is under construction

Property questions are determined by the interpreted document structure and fill in the blank questions are determined by removing the key words from sentences in your image.
